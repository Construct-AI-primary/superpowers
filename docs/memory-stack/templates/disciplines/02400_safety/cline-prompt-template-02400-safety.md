# 02400 Safety Discipline Prompt Template

## Overview

This specialized template provides a standardized structure for prompts related to the 02400 Safety discipline, leveraging the full power of the Construct AI memory system with Gigabrain integration. Use this template for all Health, Safety, Security, and Environment (HSSE) tasks including risk assessment, incident investigation, contractor safety management, compliance monitoring, and safety training.

## Discipline Context

### 02400 Safety Scope
The 02400 Safety discipline encompasses:
- **Risk Management**: Hazard identification, assessment, and mitigation strategies
- **Incident Investigation**: Root cause analysis, corrective actions, and prevention
- **Contractor Safety**: Pre-qualification, onboarding, performance management
- **HSE Compliance**: Regulatory adherence, audit procedures, certification tracking
- **Safety Training**: Competency management, awareness programs, certification
- **Emergency Response**: Crisis management, evacuation procedures, incident response
- **Equipment Safety**: Machinery guarding, PPE management, maintenance safety
- **HSSE Questionnaires**: Supplier evaluation, compliance assessment forms

### Related Disciplines
- **00850 Civil Engineering**: Construction site safety, excavation safety
- **00860 Electrical Engineering**: Electrical safety, lockout/tagout procedures
- **00870 Mechanical Engineering**: Equipment safety, machinery guarding
- **02200 Quality Assurance**: Safety-quality integration, compliance audits
- **01900 Procurement**: Contractor pre-qualification, safety certifications
- **01000 Environmental**: Environmental compliance, spill response
- **00885 HSE Director**: Executive oversight, policy governance

## Safety-Specific Best Practices

### Risk Assessment Best Practices
1. **Use Standardized Methodologies**: Apply consistent risk matrix and classification systems
2. **Involve Stakeholders**: Include workers, supervisors, and specialists in assessments
3. **Document Thoroughly**: Capture all hazards, controls, and rationale
4. **Review Regularly**: Update assessments based on changes and incidents
5. **Verify Effectiveness**: Confirm controls are implemented and working

### Incident Investigation Best Practices
1. **Respond Promptly**: Begin investigation within 24 hours for serious incidents
2. **Preserve Evidence**: Protect scene and collect physical evidence
3. **Interview Widely**: Gather perspectives from all involved parties
4. **Analyze Systematically**: Use structured root cause analysis methods
5. **Implement Effectively**: Track corrective actions to completion

### Contractor Safety Best Practices
1. **Pre-qualify Thoroughly**: Verify safety programs and performance history
2. **Orient Comprehensively**: Ensure understanding of site-specific hazards
3. **Monitor Continuously**: Track leading and lagging safety indicators
4. **Communicate Regularly**: Maintain open safety dialogue
5. **Evaluate Objectively**: Use consistent performance criteria

### Compliance Management Best Practices
1. **Stay Current**: Monitor regulatory changes and updates
2. **Document Systematically**: Maintain complete compliance records
3. **Audit Regularly**: Conduct periodic compliance assessments
4. **Correct Promptly**: Address non-conformances quickly
5. **Improve Continuously**: Enhance compliance processes

### Quality Indicators
- **Comprehensiveness**: All relevant hazards and controls identified

- **Compliance**: Alignment with regulatory requirements

## Safety-Specific Troubleshooting

### If Risk Assessment Seems Incomplete
```
Request: "Expand the risk assessment by checking related engineering disciplines for technical hazard context, reviewing similar past assessments, and verifying all applicable regulations are considered."
```

### If Incident Investigation Lacks Depth
```
Specify: "Deepen the investigation by applying structured root cause analysis (5 Whys, Fishbone), interviewing additional witnesses, reviewing similar past incidents, and analyzing systemic factors."
```

### If Contractor Safety Management Is Unclear
```
Clarify: "Reference the contractor safety management framework, check pre-qualification criteria, review onboarding requirements, and verify performance monitoring metrics."
```

### If Compliance Status Is Uncertain
```
Verify: "Cross-reference with regulatory requirements, check audit findings, review corrective action status, and confirm certification validity."
```
