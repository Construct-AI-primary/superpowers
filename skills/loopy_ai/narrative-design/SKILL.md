# Narrative Design

## Overview

**Core principle:** Craft compelling, adaptive narratives that engage audiences through sophisticated storytelling techniques, character development, and emotional resonance while leveraging AI for enhanced creativity and personalization.

**Specialization:** Loopy AI's narrative design focuses on AI-augmented storytelling that maintains human emotional depth while enabling dynamic, personalized narrative experiences.

## When to Use This Skill

**Trigger Conditions:**
- Developing story-driven content or experiences
- Creating character arcs and emotional journeys
- Designing interactive or branching narratives
- Building narrative frameworks for creative projects
- When stories need to adapt to user preferences or contexts
- For projects requiring emotional engagement and storytelling
- When combining traditional narrative with AI enhancement
- For multi-platform storytelling across different media

**Prerequisites:**
- Understanding of narrative structure and storytelling principles
- Knowledge of character development and emotional arcs
- Experience with creative writing and narrative techniques
- Familiarity with interactive storytelling systems

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Weak Premise**: Starting with underdeveloped or uncompelling core ideas
2. **Flat Characters**: Creating one-dimensional characters without emotional depth
3. **Poor Pacing**: Rushing important moments or dragging uneventful sections
4. **Inconsistent Tone**: Failing to maintain narrative voice and style consistency
5. **Forced Interactivity**: Adding choices that don't meaningfully impact the story
6. **Ethical Oversights**: Not properly disclosing or managing AI narrative contributions
7. **Cultural Insensitivity**: Ignoring diverse audience perspectives and backgrounds
8. **Technical Limitations**: Designing narratives that exceed platform capabilities
