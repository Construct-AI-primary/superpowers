# DomainForge AI Skills

## Overview

DomainForge AI specializes in industry-specific AI solutions and domain expertise integration. This directory contains skills tailored for vertical market applications and specialized business domains.

## Focus Areas

- **Industry-Specific AI Models** - Custom models for healthcare, finance, legal, etc.
- **Domain Expertise Integration** - Incorporating subject matter expert knowledge
- **Regulatory Compliance** - Domain-specific compliance and governance
- **Vertical Solutions** - End-to-end solutions for specific industries

## Skill Categories

### Healthcare AI
- Medical data processing and analysis
- Clinical decision support systems
- HIPAA-compliant AI implementations

### Financial AI
- Risk assessment and fraud detection
- Algorithmic trading strategies
- Regulatory reporting automation

### Legal AI
- Contract analysis and review
- Legal research automation
- Compliance monitoring

### Manufacturing AI
- Predictive maintenance
- Quality control automation
- Supply chain optimization

## Getting Started

Skills in this folder are specific to DomainForge AI implementations. For cross-platform skills, see the `shared/` directory.
